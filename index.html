Enhanced ToDo App

This app is used to manage your tasks and be able to analyze your progress over time. People can register, add/delete tasks, filter tasks by seach and date, and observe their data. One can make this app a tool if they consistently add tasks, update them, and look at data regarding their task completion. By doing this, an individual can compare their efficieny over time and determine if they are improving or not. Also, there is a home page where one can post their short, mid, and long-term goals in different categories to keep individuals accountable.

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Django, AdobeXD

Chef Stef for MS

Fundraising platform for Multiple Sclerosis. This app allows users to create an account, publish their own recipes, and purchase other people's recipes in order to donate money towards MS. On this platform users are also able to follow other users, like recipes, and view the top 5 most viewed recipes. There is also a dashboard that shows activity from people that a user follows.

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Django, AdobeXD
